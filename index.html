ice protection requirements

sneakers
It is a high-waisted type, and the outer layers such as the toe cap, upper, two ankles, and heel are all hard. A long front tongue with a firm high waist wraps the leg and ankle for support and strength. Ice hockey shoes were originally made of high-quality cowhide, and all-plastic molded shoes appeared in the 1960s and 1970s. Now the international use of nylon fiber upper, plastic bottom hockey shoes. These shoes are lighter than leather shoes, hard and moisture-resistant, and are suitable for indoor ice rinks.
ball knife
Originally a Tito steel blade, now more plastic knife holders are used, high-quality alloy steel blades, which have the advantages of light weight, anti-strike, and not easy to rust. Ice hockey knives are tall and short, with large arcs and thicker blades. The height of the blade prevents the shoes from touching the ice surface when the athlete makes a sharp turn; the blade has a large arc, and the contact area with the ice surface is small, so it can flexibly slide and change direction; the blade is thick and can resist blows without bending; With shallow grooves to make it sharp and durable. The goalkeeper skates are wrapped with special thickened hard leather around the shoes to resist the ball and protect the feet. The goalkeeper skate is quite different from the athlete’s skate. It is all made of metal.
protective gear
In order to prevent injury in the intense confrontation, athletes wear protective gear all over the body. Protective gear includes helmets, face masks, shoulder pads, chest pads, waist pads, body pads, elbow pads, gloves, pants, leg pads, ankle pads, etc. Modern ice hockey protective gear generally adopts light rigid plastic shell, lined with sponge or foam soft pad. Goalkeepers wear special masks, gloves, thickened chest guards and thickened and widened leg guards.
club
Made of wood material, the length from the root to the shank end should not exceed 147 cm. The blade of the rod should not be longer than 32 cm, and the width should be 5-7.5 cm. The widened part of the shank of the goalkeeper’s club shall not be longer than 71 cm from the root upwards and not more than 9 cm wide, and the length of the blade shall not exceed 39 cm and the width shall not exceed 9 cm. In order to reduce the weight, there are clubs made of carbon materials, which reduce the weight while keeping the length and width unchanged, making it easier for players to play.

book of hockey

The Origins of Ice Hockey was co-authored by three members of the International Society for Hockey Research (SIHR). Originally initiated by full-time computer analyst Martel, and later joined by Swedes Carl Kiden (doctor) and Patrick Gouda (reporter), they uncovered the birthplace of ice hockey through extensive research. the truth.

As mentioned earlier, their research revealed that the first game may have been traced back to the 1890s.

They found that the name “hockey puck” may have come from the corks they used to serve as pucks. Corks were used as stoppers for beer kegs, and Hock Ale was a popular drink at the time, according to Adam Proteau.

Another shocking discovery is that Charles Darwin was a well-known figure in science and evolution, but no one would associate the famous scientist with ice hockey. However, three SIHR researchers found a link, also including King Edward VII and Prince Albert (Queen Victoria’s princely consort) as two of the earliest participants in the sport.

So how did Darwin get involved with ice hockey?

The researchers discovered something in a letter from Darwin to his son William dated March 1, 1853, in which he wrote: “My dear William…do you have a pond where you can skate? I used to like it very much. Play hockey while skating on the ice.”

Darwin was actually referencing the games he played in Shrewsborough, England, east of the Welsh border in the 1820s. While some might think “Hocky” is a misprint, it’s not. This is exactly what was written in 19th century England. In fact, the reference here is not hockey at all, but a game of ice throwing.

So if the Montreal game in 1875 wasn’t the first ice hockey game, and Darwin’s game in the 1820s wasn’t the first, how far back do we go back to the first game?

Ice hockey skills

  1. An ice hockey team has 6 players (in fact, each team can be composed of 20 players, but only 6 players are allowed).

  2. Referees of ice hockey matches: referees include two on-site referees and two goal supervisors (goal referees). Two line referees, a timekeeper and a scorer. On the court, the referees jointly control the whole game, each responsible for a half court. The sideline referee is mainly responsible for signaling when someone is offside.

  3. There are several foul actions in ice hockey. Ice hockey is a game in which physical contact occurs. There are many possibilities of fouls on the other party. Fouls include hitting, tripping, pushing people with knees, pushing people with elbows, raising the rod over the shoulder and poking people with ice hockey sticks.

  4. How to kick off in an ice hockey game: the game kicks off in the ring. The referee stood in the center circle and dropped the ball between two opposite centers.

Ice hockey training skills

  1. the first is the station. Instead of facing the goal, face the puck sideways toward the goal, with legs shoulder-width apart for balance and knees slightly bent.
  2. Move the ball some distance in the opposite direction of the shot, taking the most comfortable distance for each person as the standard.
  3. After moving, the nose and toe should be on the same vertical plane, that is, the nose tip corresponds to the toe. Center of gravity on the foot far from the goal, should not be too much to the direction of the puck movement, so easy to lose balance.
  4. Move the puck toward the goal. Again, the tip of the nose is in the same vertical plane as the tip of the foot in front.
  5. while moving the puck, turn your foot in front of the goal, and rotate your body to open up so that your whole body is facing the goal. The club sends the ball forward as well as the body forward, noting that both the hand and club are in front of the body.
  6. At the moment of hitting, the puck is hit out with the help of the whole arm and the power of the waist and abdomen.
  7. Break down the action. We put 4steps together and a shot is done. Pay attention to the Angle of the shot. Shoot high or low as needed.

A strong ice hockey country: Canada

Ice hockey is one of the most exciting team sports in the world and, of course, one of the fastest. The crazy sport is a combination of skill, balance and physical strength. It’s a high-speed, crash-filled event and one of the most entertaining events at the Winter Olympics. The National Stadium will host all of the men’s ice hockey events at the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics, some of the women’s ice hockey finals and some of the ice skating events of the Winter Paralympics. At the same time, Canada, the traditional power of the hockey world and the team that has won more gold MEDALS than any other in the sport, will once again defend its supremacy. Ice hockey made its Olympic debut in 1920 at the Antwerp Summer Games. The winbourne Falcons of Manitoba won the gold medal for Canada. Ice hockey was added to the first Winter Olympics in Chamonix, France, in 1924. It was the beginning of Canada’s reign in ice hockey. The country that originated the sport, Canada had won the previous three Winter Olympics and five of the previous six. In particular, at the 1924 Games in Chamonix, Canada produced a series of phenomenal results, beating Czechoslovakia 30-0, Sweden 22-0, and Switzerland 33-0. The results were jaw-dropping: Canada scored 85 goals while its opponents failed to score any. In the final leg, Canada saw off Great Britain (19-2) and the United States (6-1) to claim the title, thus securing a memorable Winter Olympics title. One of them, Harold Ellis Harry Watson, captivated the public with his consistent performances and set a record that will probably never be broken. Watson, nicknamed ‘Moose’, was a flying ace in the Royal Flying Corps during world War I. The 26-year-old has made an indelible mark on winter sports because he seems to be able to score at will. In this match against Czechoslovakia, he scored an astonishing 11 goals. And that wasn’t all. In his later showdown with the Swiss, the skilful Ontario native set an Olympic record of 13 goals in one game. Watson’s scoring prowess is not limited to underdogs. He scored half of Canada’s goals in a 6-1 rout of rival U.S. team USA in the final, bringing his total to 36 goals in five games. So much so that canadians say of hockey, “It’s our game.”

Story of Ice hockey

Ice hockey originated in Canada in the mid-19th century. A popular ice game in Kingston, Canada, involves players chasing and slapping wooden hockey puck across a frozen lake with skates strapped to their feet and sticks in hand. At that time, the number of participants and the venue were unlimited, only two wooden poles as the goal, which is the predecessor of modern ice hockey. In 1879 McGill University in Montreal, Canada organized an official ice hockey match under the rules of W.F. Professor Robertson and Professor R.F. Smith. Initially 11 participants from each side, then nine, then seven, and finally six. Clubs and balls have also been improved. Later, the game gradually spread to the United States and Europe. The International Ice Hockey Federation was founded in 1908 in Paris and is headquartered in Vienna, Austria. Ice hockey was included in the seventh Olympic Games in 1920. In 1924, the first Winter Olympics were held in France, and The Canadian team won the gold medal by a landslide. From 1924 to 1953, Canada often took the lead in the world ice hockey, winning the world championship many times. In 1954, the Soviet Union defeated Canada to win the 21st World Hockey Championship, breaking Canada’s dominance in ice hockey. Ice hockey has a history of more than 60 years in China. In 1935, the first ice hockey match was held at the first North China Ice Games In Peiping. After the founding of the People’s Republic of China, ice hockey developed rapidly. In 1953, the first National Ice Games were held in Harbin. Five teams participated in the ice hockey competition. Later in the northeast, north China and other provinces, cities have established various forms of ice hockey teams. National ice hockey games have been held annually since 1955. After 1956, The Chinese ice hockey team began to participate in international competitions. In 1981, Beijing hosted the World Ice Hockey Group C championship, and The Chinese team won the second place and was promoted to Group B. The Chinese team won the gold medal in the first Asian Winter Games held in Japan in 1986.

Ice hockey is a sport that combines the techniques and tactical ideas of football, hockey and speed skating. It was invented a little later than football, hockey and speed skating.

Ice hockey requires agile and skillful curling skills, fast and changeable skating skills and collective cooperation consciousness. It is a team sport with strong physical antagonism.

It is recorded that before the British and French settlers and British colonialists came to Canada, the native Indians were already playing an interesting game on the ice. Later, after the British colonizers occupied North America, the British soldiers in Canada were inspired by the Indians. In their spare time, they often hit cans on ice.

When the ice activities of various countries are still in the game stage, The Canadian student Wu Robertson used the hockey method learned during the study in The UK on the ice, and combined with the characteristics of the Indian “La Crosse” ball, created a new type of ice sport, which is the embryonic form of ice hockey.

In 1858, the movement spread rapidly from Montreal and Quebec to the whole of Canada. Therefore, the original game of ice hockey is also called “Canadian ball”. In 1875, simple rules for ice hockey were established. 1879 Ice hockey began. 1890 The first ice hockey organization, the Hockey Association of Ontario, was formed.

Since then, the sport spread to Europe and countries around the world and popular.
