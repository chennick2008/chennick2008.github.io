Ice hockey dairies

Today, my friends and I came to Suzhou center. There is a skating rink on the fourth floor. We haven’t skated before. We all want to have a try.

After buying the tickets, we went in to change our shoes. Change your shoes and enter the venue. At first, I couldn’t slide at all. I looked left and right to see how they slipped. I saw some people divide their legs into ‘eight’ shapes, spread their hands to the side and slide forward easily. Some put their hands behind their backs, while others are beginners like me. I thought to myself: what’s the difficulty? It’s just sliding around! It’s a piece of cake for me. I learn from others and slide like a mold. But I didn’t expect that as soon as I slipped, I fell and a dog bit the mud. Ah! Unfortunately, thanks to the protective sleeve, it doesn’t hurt much. It turns out that skating is not as simple as I thought. I didn’t lose heart at all and got up again. Because I believe: painstaking people live up to heaven, where there is a will, there is a way. Finally, I learned to skate when I fell and got up again and again. At that time, don’t mention how happy and excited I was! I can finally fly like a bird in the open spaceBy the shuttle, it feels great, not to mention. My friend’s brother is skating, too,I don’t know how many times faster than me. I dragged his clothes and he slipped. As soon as I let go, I fell again.After skating for two hours, we went out. After changing shoes, walking is very painful. But I feel very happy and worth it.
