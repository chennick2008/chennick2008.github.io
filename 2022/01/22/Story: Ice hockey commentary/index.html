Story: Ice hockey commentary

Once Liu Yapeng commentated on a game about the penguins. Due to the wonderful performance of the players he personally appreciated, Liu Yapeng himself commentated passionately. Later, he was praised by the penguins’ fans for being objective and neutral. He was happy, but his heart was beating, because penguins fans praised him for being objective and neutral, which probably meant that the commentary wasn’t. So he immediately went back and listened to the commentary twice, reviewing what might have rattled a fan’s nerves and taking notes to determine what not to say. At the end of last year, Liu Yapeng encountered a problem in the course of interpretation guru Yu Jia – what on earth are we explaining for the audience. Liu yapeng’s own answer is that we are explaining for the audience’s emotions, but also not only for the audience’s emotions. If only to explain the audience’s emotions, the commentator may fall into the situation of emotional outburst by hook or by hook, but deviate from objectivity and fairness. And this is liu Yapeng in the process of commentary hockey commentary this job a little bit of thinking.
